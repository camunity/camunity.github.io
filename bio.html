Biography

Socially conscious and enthusiastic about creating a brighter future through open-dialogue, and collaboration Cameron has heavy interests in mobile technology and music as mediums for facilitating cross-cultural communication. An avid writer, musician, and developer, Cam is the self-ascribed "Hip Hop Programmer" and is using all of his various platforms to educate and empower all people. He thoroughly enjoys group discussion, teamwork, and joint projects geared toward social change and as Co-Founder of IncluDe, a tech startup offering minority entrepreneurs and developers a necessary space to meet and work together on web and mobile software; Cameron is preparing himself as a social entrepreneur to diversify the technology sector as it currently stands. He values the power of education and mentorship, and hopes to teach Computer Science in primary and secondary education in the city of Chicago.
